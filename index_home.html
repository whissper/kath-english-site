Programs

  • 01 Orientation Session

    – it's free
    – lasts 20-30 minutes
    – focuses on your goals
    – a short program discussion
    – your questions

  • 02 Diagnostic Coaching Session

    – it's free
    – lasts around 60 minutes
    – includes English level assessment
    – goals setting
    – strategy development
    – time frame setting
    – establishing frequency of sessions

  • 03 Individual Language Coaching

    • Conducting sessions in accordance with the client's goals, based on a jointly developed lesson plan.
    • Conducting a coaching session to identify negative mindset and mental blocks that interfere with the material assimilation, as well as goal review and new goals setting.

    Common queries:
    – prepare for an interview in English
    – learn how to make presentations in English
    – Business English: negotiations and business correspondence, foreign trade contracts, law, marketing, HR
    – prepare for IELTS General or Academic
    – "stuck" at the intermediate level and can't improve your English fluency
    – don't feel comfortable with “traditional” approach to teaching

  • 04 Group Language Coaching

    – Group lessons in General and Spoken English with goal setting and team work to achieve them.
    – Study groups are formed according to the level of proficiency.

    * Please contact me to check for availability.

  • 05 Group IELTS Preparation

    IELTS Express Course "Get IELTS Band 7.0 and higher in 4 months" for Upper-Intermediate students.

    The course focuses on 4 parts of the exam with extra attention to Speaking and Writing.

    The course includes:
    – 1 combined session per week for IELTS General and Academic;
    – 1 separate session per week to practise Reading and Writing Part 1;
    – group chat with my support;
    – answers to your questions;
    – weekly correction and evaluation of your written assignments.

    * Please contact me to check for availability.
